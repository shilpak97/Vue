    POSMART (Point Of Sale)

    This software project is a online solution for traditional supermarket billing system with multiplatform and some other added functionality . This system is built for fast data processing and bill generation for supermarket customers. The billing database is a vast collection of product name,Customer details, price and other product specific data. A product when billed is searched from the database and its price is added to the bill based upon the product quantity and the available discount is applied on the product. Various payment modes are available for the customer and details of the customer are recorded for future reference. The application has the feature of inventory management to manage the flow of goods.
